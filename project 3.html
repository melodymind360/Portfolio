Joker Poster Design

This is a project I did for a graphic design class using corel draw


The process of making the poster included me to first find pictures of the joker that I liked, I recently read the comic death in the family to i lookjed for images from that comic for one the images i used, i also stuck to the same artist or ones with similar art styles in order to have similar designs. Once i found the images I wanted I put them into corel draw and used the pen tool to trace around the images, or cropped them, I then played with transparacy and the color contrast of the images to make them blend in together.
